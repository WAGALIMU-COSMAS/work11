SCHOOL VISION

    See neglected and vulnerable children leading this country throuogh quality education and to be a leading first world school in the fishing community.

SCHOOL MISSION

    To teach and educate needy children in the fishing communities and to provide quality education to the left out communities in the Lake Victoria Uganda.

SCHOOL CORE VALUES

    Fearing God, Respect and Discipline, Compassion, Integrity, Environmental Protection, Sanitation and Hygiene, Expression in English, Self Sustenance through Agriculture.

ISLAND MISION UGANDA

Island Mission Uganda(IMU)is an NGO which was founded in 2010, Its funded by whites from different European Countries just as proclaimed in our "About" above. It helps through providing income to facilitate and run the project activities such as:

  1. Paying teachers salaries
  2. Purchasing all neccessary materials that aid in proper running of the project activities.
  3. Funding Agriculture where we get food the the entire project population feed.
  4. Enhancing the infrastructures such as building classes, offices and other buildings relevant for the the project use.
  5. Paying wages to the relevant workers like garden attendants, cooks, askari, canteen attendant, compound cleaners among others.
